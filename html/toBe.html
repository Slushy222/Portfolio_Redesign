TO BE, TO HAVE, TO MATURE, 2024–2025

Three TV programming installation
Made in collaboration with Kailey Agoo

Watching us, it scours our world for any semblance of understanding, shifting through a cacophony of objects desperately seeking a representation of itself.

A cell with no neighbors. Loneliness.

A cell with neighbors. Contentment.

A cell with too many neighbors. Drained.

Loneliness, balance, and overcrowding—the cell is emergent. Rules guide its thinking. It knows us, it's known us, it is us.
