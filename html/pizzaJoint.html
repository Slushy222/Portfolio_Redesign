Five Wit's Pizza Joint, 2026

Branding, External Signage, & Menu Design

As a new entry into the Monen Family Resturant Group's portfolio, Five Wit's Pizza Joint aimed to deliver a new envigorating expirience to the brewery's older menu. With the location's dormant music venue during the day, the owner Mike Monen wanted to bring life to the front of Five Wits by bringing the Pizza Joint's branding more street facing. In collaboation with fellow designer, Mark Walter, we crafted an identity for Five Wit's food offerings that had to both: feel at home with the existing parent brand, while also bringing excitment to a new food venture. Pizza is crispy and its a great little spot in the heart of Main Street!
