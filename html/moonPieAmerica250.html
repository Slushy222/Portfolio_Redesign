MoonPie America 250th, 2026

8.75 x 4.25 x 3.65in.
Limited edition MoonPie 10CT packaging for America's 250th
Made with Whiteboard

To celebrate America's 250th, MoonPie wanted us to explore an alternate look to the Chattanoogan classic. Instead of the traditional box design with a solid color and a clear Pie, we presented an alternative design opting for a Pie that would be in conversation with neighboring shelved boxes, completing the American color scheme on the shelf. This allowed for both stars and stripes respectively to have their own flavors.
