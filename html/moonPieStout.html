MoonPie Stout, 2025

8.25 x 2.625in.
Stout beer can wrapper

From the old Chattanoogan classic, MoonPie jumped into a new market with its second installment of their chocolate stout. From remixing the old classic, these treatments focused on expanding MoonPie's voice just as the product was attempting to expand its reach to its audience. With their roots firmly planted in an Americana look and feel, these designs never made the light of day, but they shouldn't sit in file folders forever!
